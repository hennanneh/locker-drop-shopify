## General Questions

### What is LockerDrop?

LockerDrop is a Shopify app that adds smart locker pickup as an option at checkout. You personally drop off orders at a nearby Harbor Locker location, and customers pick them up at their convenience. It's not a shipping carrier — it's a self-managed pickup service ideal for local sellers.

### How does it work?

1. Customer selects "LockerDrop Pickup" at checkout (on Shopify Plus, they choose a specific locker; on Basic plans, a nearby location is auto-assigned)
2. A locker is **reserved immediately** when they complete payment
3. You receive the order with a drop-off link
4. You can change the locker location from the dashboard if needed (before drop-off)
5. You drop the package at the assigned locker using your phone
6. Customer gets notified automatically and picks up using their link

### What are Harbor Lockers?

Harbor Lockers are secure, automated pickup lockers located in convenient public locations like apartment buildings, retail stores, and office complexes. Each locker has a touchscreen where users enter access codes to open compartments.

### Is LockerDrop free for customers?

That depends on your settings! You can configure LockerDrop to be:
- **Free** (most common) - as a convenient pickup option
- **Discounted** - lower cost than other options
- **Standard rate** - same as other checkout options

### What happens if lockers are full?

If no lockers of the required size are available, checkout will **not allow** the customer to select LockerDrop. This prevents situations where an order is placed but no locker can be assigned. The customer will need to choose another option or wait until lockers become available.

### Can lockers get "oversold"?

No! Unlike some systems that just check availability, LockerDrop actually **reserves the locker during checkout** before the customer pays. This guarantees a locker is available for every LockerDrop order.

## For Sellers

### How do I get started?

1. Install the LockerDrop app from the Shopify App Store
2. Select which locker locations you want to use
3. Configure your product sizes
4. LockerDrop will appear as a pickup option at checkout

### Do I need special equipment?

No! You just need:
- A smartphone or computer to access the dashboard
- Your packages ready to drop off
- Transportation to reach locker locations

### How do I know when I have a new order?

- Email notification (if enabled)
- Dashboard shows "Pending Drop-offs" count
- Orders tab lists all orders needing attention

### What if I can't drop off today?

That's okay! Orders don't have immediate deadlines. However:
- Customers expect reasonable fulfillment times
- Set realistic processing times in your settings
- Communicate delays to customers when needed

### Can I use LockerDrop for all my products?

Most products work great! Consider:
- **Size** - Must fit in largest locker (24" x 20" x 16")
- **Value** - High-value items are secure in lockers
- **Fragility** - Lockers are safe for most items

Products that may NOT work:
- Oversized items (furniture, large equipment)
- Temperature-sensitive items (unless using special lockers)
- Extremely heavy items

### How much does LockerDrop cost me?

LockerDrop is free to install. You only pay a small locker pickup fee each time a customer uses locker pickup at checkout. No monthly subscription, no setup fees. You only pay when you're making sales.

### Do customers choose their own locker location?

It depends on your Shopify plan:
- **Shopify Plus:** Customers see an interactive locker picker at checkout and choose their preferred location.
- **Basic / Standard Shopify:** The checkout UI extension isn't available, so a nearby locker location is **auto-assigned** based on the customer's shipping address. You can easily change the assigned location from your dashboard — just open the order, click **"Change"** next to the locker location, and select a different one.

### Can I use multiple locker locations?

Yes! In the "My Lockers" tab, you can select any Harbor Locker locations you want to use. Most sellers choose 3-10 locations that are convenient for them and their customers.

### How do I check real-time locker availability?

Go to the **Availability** tab in your dashboard. You'll see:
- Each enabled locker location
- Available lockers by size (small, medium, large)
- Total capacity and current usage
- Last updated timestamp

Click "Refresh" to get the latest availability from Harbor Lockers.

## Orders & Fulfillment

### How do I find the locker location?

Each order includes:
- Locker name (e.g., "Harbor Locker - Downtown Dallas")
- Full street address
- Sometimes specific instructions (e.g., "Inside lobby")

### What if the package doesn't fit?

**From the dashboard:**
- Open the order and click **"Change Size"** to select a different locker size
- A new drop-off link will be generated automatically

**At the locker:**
- Select "doesn't fit" on the Harbor screen — you'll be guided to choose a larger size
- Never force a package into a locker

### Can I drop off multiple orders at once?

Yes! If you have multiple orders for the same locker location:
- Each order has its own code
- Drop off each package separately using its specific code
- Each package goes in its own compartment

### What happens if I use the wrong code?

- The system won't let you open the wrong locker
- Double-check your codes before entering
- Contact support if you're having issues

### How long do customers have to pick up?

Default is **5 days** from when you mark the order as dropped off. After that:
- Package is flagged for return
- Customer is notified of expiration
- You coordinate on next steps

### What if a customer doesn't pick up?

1. You'll receive a notification
2. Package may be returned to a holding location
3. Options:
   - Reship to a new locker (additional fee may apply)
   - Arrange alternative delivery
   - Process a refund

## Customer Questions

*These are questions your customers may ask you*

### "Where is my locker?"

Direct them to check their confirmation email, which includes:
- Locker location name
- Full address
- Pickup code

### "I lost my pickup code!"

1. Go to the Orders tab
2. Find their order
3. Click "Resend Pickup Email"
4. They'll receive a new email with the code

### "The locker won't open!"

Ask them to verify:
1. They're at the correct location
2. They're using the PICKUP code (starts with PICK-)
3. The code hasn't expired

If still not working, contact Harbor support.

### "Can I change the locker location?"

- **Before drop-off:** Yes! Open the order in the dashboard, click **"Change"** next to the locker location, select a new location, and click **"Move Order"**. A new drop-off link is generated automatically.
- **After drop-off:** No, the package must be picked up at the assigned location.
- **Note:** On Basic Shopify plans, customers can't select a locker at checkout — a location is auto-assigned based on their address. Sellers can change the assigned location from the dashboard at any time before drop-off.

### "How long will my order be held?"

Standard hold time is 5 days. After that, packages may be returned.

### "Is my package safe in the locker?"

Yes! Harbor Lockers are:
- Secure, steel compartments
- Access-code protected
- Under 24/7 video surveillance
- Weather-protected

### "Can someone else pick up for me?"

Yes, as long as they have the pickup code. The locker doesn't verify identity - just the correct code.

## Technical Issues

### "Dashboard won't load"

1. Clear your browser cache
2. Try a different browser
3. Make sure you're logged into Shopify
4. Check your internet connection
5. Contact support if still not working

### "I'm not getting email notifications"

1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Verify email address in Notifications settings
3. Make sure notifications are enabled
4. Add support@lockerdrop.it to your contacts

### "Locker list isn't loading"

1. Refresh the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. The Harbor API may be temporarily unavailable
4. Try again in a few minutes

### "Order status isn't updating"

1. Refresh your browser
2. Changes may take a moment to sync
3. Try logging out and back in
4. Contact support if the issue persists

## Billing & Account

### How do I update my payment method?

Billing is handled through Shopify's built-in billing system. You can view your usage in the Billing tab of the LockerDrop dashboard.

### Can I pause my LockerDrop service?

Since LockerDrop only charges when customers use locker pickup, there are no monthly fees to pause. Simply stop offering locker pickup and you won't incur any charges. If you need to temporarily disable the service:
- Disable LockerDrop in your settings
- Existing orders must still be fulfilled
- Re-enabling is instant

### How do I cancel LockerDrop?

Contact support@lockerdrop.it. Before canceling:
- Complete all pending orders
- Download any order history you need
- Allow 30 days for cancellation processing
