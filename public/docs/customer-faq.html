Everything you need to know about picking up your order from a locker

What is LockerDrop?

LockerDrop lets you pick up your order from a secure smart locker instead of waiting at home for delivery. Choose a convenient location at checkout, and we'll notify you when your package is ready to pick up!

Why Choose LockerDrop?

  • Convenience - Pick up when YOU want, not when the delivery truck arrives
  • Security - No packages left on your doorstep
  • Flexible - Located near where you live, work, or shop
  • Free or low cost - Often cheaper than home delivery

Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering

Where are the lockers located?

Locker locations vary by seller. Common locations include:

  • Apartment building lobbies
  • Retail stores
  • Office buildings
  • Grocery stores
  • Shopping centers

You'll see available locations during checkout based on your address.

What size packages can the lockers hold?

Lockers come in different sizes:

  • Small: Jewelry, small accessories
  • Medium: Clothing, shoes
  • Large: Multiple items, larger boxes
  • X-Large: Bulk orders, big packages

If your order is too large for lockers, LockerDrop may not be available at checkout.

Is LockerDrop available for all products?

Most products are available for LockerDrop! Some items may be excluded:

  • Very large items
  • Items requiring signature
  • Temperature-sensitive products

Pickup

How long do I have to pick up my package?

Typically 5 days from when you receive the "Ready for Pickup" notification. Check your email for the exact deadline.

Can I pick up anytime?

Most locker locations are accessible 24/7! Some locations inside businesses may have limited hours. Your confirmation email will include any access restrictions.

What if I can't pick up in time?

If you can't pick up before the deadline:

  • Contact the seller as soon as possible
  • They may be able to extend the hold time
  • Or arrange an alternative delivery method
Can someone else pick up for me?

Yes! Just share your pickup link with them. The locker opens for anyone with the correct link.

I lost my pickup link! What do I do?

Contact the seller - they can resend the pickup email with your link.

At the Locker

How do I find the locker?

Your confirmation email includes:

  • Locker location name
  • Full street address
  • Sometimes specific directions (e.g., "Inside main lobby")

Harbor Lockers are large, silver kiosks with a touchscreen display.

My link isn't working!

Double-check:

  1. You're at the correct locker location
  2. You're clicking the pickup link from your email
  3. The pickup deadline hasn't passed

Still not working? Contact the seller or Harbor support.

The locker is damaged or won't open!
  • Don't try to force it open
  • Contact the seller immediately
  • They'll work with Harbor to resolve the issue

After Pickup

My package was damaged!

Contact the seller with photos of the damage. Packages are insured during the locker storage period.

Something is missing from my order!

Contact the seller with your order number. They'll investigate and make it right.

Can I return items through the locker?

Not at this time. Follow the seller's standard return policy for returns.

Troubleshooting

I never received my pickup notification
  1. Check your spam/junk folder
  2. Make sure you entered the correct email at checkout
  3. Contact the seller to verify order status and resend the email
I arrived but there's no locker there
  1. Double-check the address in your email
  2. Look for the Harbor Locker - it may be inside the building
  3. Ask building staff for help locating it
  4. Contact the seller if you still can't find it

Tips for a Great Experience

  1. Choose a convenient location - Pick a locker near your home, work, or daily route
  2. Check the deadline - Note when you need to pick up by
  3. Save the email - Keep your confirmation email handy for the address and link
  4. Check locker hours - Some locations have restricted access times
  5. Pick up promptly - Don't wait until the last minute in case of issues

Contact

Having trouble? Contact the seller directly:

  • Check your order confirmation email for seller contact info
  • Or reach out through the store's contact page

For locker technical issues, contact Harbor Locker support.
