General Questions

What is LockerDrop?

LockerDrop is a Shopify app that adds smart locker pickup as a shipping option. Customers can choose to pick up their orders from secure Harbor Locker locations instead of home delivery.

How does it work?
  1. Customer selects "LockerDrop Pickup" at checkout
  2. A locker is reserved immediately when they complete payment
  3. You receive the order with a drop-off link
  4. You drop the package at the assigned locker using your phone
  5. Customer gets notified automatically and picks up using their link
What are Harbor Lockers?

Harbor Lockers are secure, automated pickup lockers located in convenient public locations like apartment buildings, retail stores, and office complexes. Each locker has a touchscreen where users can open compartments using secure links.

Is LockerDrop free for customers?

That depends on your settings! You can configure LockerDrop to be:

  • Free (most common) - as a delivery alternative
  • Discounted - cheaper than home delivery
  • Standard rate - same as other shipping options
What happens if lockers are full?

If no lockers of the required size are available, checkout will not allow the customer to select LockerDrop. This prevents situations where an order is placed but no locker can be assigned. The customer will need to choose regular shipping or wait until lockers become available.

Can lockers get "oversold"?

No! Unlike some systems that just check availability, LockerDrop actually reserves the locker during checkout before the customer pays. This guarantees a locker is available for every LockerDrop order.

For Sellers

How do I get started?
  1. Install the LockerDrop app from the Shopify App Store
  2. Select which locker locations you want to use
  3. Configure your shipping settings
  4. LockerDrop will appear as a shipping option at checkout
Do I need special equipment?

No! You just need:

  • A smartphone or computer to access the dashboard
  • Your packages ready to drop off
  • Transportation to reach locker locations
How do I know when I have a new order?
  • Dashboard shows "Pending Drop-offs" count
  • Orders tab lists all orders needing attention
  • You'll see the order in your Shopify admin as well
Can I use LockerDrop for all my products?

Most products work great! Consider:

  • Size - Must fit in largest locker (24" x 20" x 16")
  • Value - High-value items are secure in lockers
  • Fragility - Lockers are safe for most items

Products that may NOT work:

  • Oversized items (furniture, large equipment)
  • Temperature-sensitive items (unless using special lockers)
  • Extremely heavy items
Can I use multiple locker locations?

Yes! In the "My Lockers" tab, you can select any Harbor Locker locations you want to use. Most sellers choose 3-10 locations that are convenient for them and their customers.

How do I check real-time locker availability?

Go to the Availability tab in your dashboard. You'll see:

  • Each enabled locker location
  • Available lockers by size (small, medium, large)
  • Total capacity and current usage
  • Last updated timestamp

Click "Refresh" to get the latest availability from Harbor Lockers.

Orders & Fulfillment

How do I find the locker location?

Each order includes:

  • Locker name (e.g., "Harbor Locker - Downtown Dallas")
  • Full street address
  • Sometimes specific instructions (e.g., "Inside lobby")
What if the package doesn't fit?

Before dropping off:

  • Contact support to reassign to a larger locker
  • Never force a package that doesn't fit

If you discover at the locker:

  • Do not leave the package
  • Return and contact support
  • We'll assign a different locker
Can I drop off multiple orders at once?

Yes! If you have multiple orders for the same locker location:

  • Each order has its own drop-off link
  • Drop off each package separately using its specific link
  • Each package goes in its own compartment
How long do customers have to pick up?

Default is 5 days from when you mark the order as dropped off. After that:

  • Package is flagged for return
  • Customer is notified of expiration
  • You coordinate on next steps
What if a customer doesn't pick up?
  1. You'll receive a notification
  2. Package may be returned to a holding location
  3. Options:
    • Reship to a new locker
    • Arrange alternative delivery
    • Process a refund

Customer Questions

These are questions your customers may ask you

"Where is my locker?"

Direct them to check their confirmation email, which includes:

  • Locker location name
  • Full address
  • Pickup link
"I lost my pickup link!"
  1. Go to the Orders tab
  2. Find their order
  3. Click "View" then "Resend Pickup Email"
  4. They'll receive a new email with the link
"The locker won't open!"

Ask them to verify:

  1. They're at the correct location
  2. They're using their PICKUP link (not the seller drop-off link)
  3. The link hasn't expired

If still not working, contact Harbor support.

"Can someone else pick up for me?"

Yes, as long as they have the pickup link. The locker doesn't verify identity - just the correct link/code.

Technical Issues

"Dashboard won't load"
  1. Clear your browser cache
  2. Try a different browser
  3. Make sure you're logged into Shopify
  4. Check your internet connection
  5. Contact support if still not working
"Locker list isn't loading"
  1. Refresh the page
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. The Harbor API may be temporarily unavailable
  4. Try again in a few minutes
"Order status isn't updating"
  1. Refresh your browser
  2. Changes may take a moment to sync
  3. Try logging out and back in
  4. Contact support if the issue persists

Billing & Account

What subscription plans are available?

LockerDrop offers three plans:

  • Basic ($9/month) - 25 orders/month, email notifications, dashboard access
  • Pro ($29/month) - 100 orders/month, email & SMS, priority support, analytics
  • Enterprise ($99/month) - Unlimited orders, custom branding, upsell widgets, Rebuy integration

All plans include a 3-day free trial.

How do I change my plan?
  1. Go to the Billing tab in your dashboard
  2. Click on the plan you want to switch to
  3. Confirm the change through Shopify's billing system
  4. Your new plan takes effect immediately
What happens if I hit my order limit?

When you approach your monthly order limit:

  • You'll see a warning in the Billing tab usage meter
  • At 90%, the meter turns red
  • Consider upgrading to avoid service interruption
Can I pause my LockerDrop service?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription in the Billing tab. Note that:

  • Your subscription remains active until the end of the billing period
  • Existing orders must still be fulfilled
  • Reactivation is quick and easy
How do I cancel LockerDrop?

Go to the Billing tab and click "Cancel Subscription". Before canceling:

  • Complete all pending orders
  • Download any order history you need

Enterprise Features

What is custom branding?

Enterprise subscribers can customize the pickup success page customers see after collecting their order:

  • Your brand logo
  • Custom colors matching your brand
  • Personalized thank-you message
  • Upsell products with links to your store
  • Rebuy widget integration
  • Custom CSS for advanced styling
How do I set up upsell products?
  1. Go to the Branding tab (Enterprise only)
  2. Enable "Show upsell products"
  3. Enter your Shopify product IDs (comma-separated, max 4)
  4. Customize the upsell heading (e.g., "You might also like")
  5. Save changes

Find product IDs in Shopify Admin: Products > [Product] > check the URL for the numeric ID.

How do I connect Rebuy?
  1. Go to the Branding tab
  2. Enable "Rebuy widget"
  3. Enter your Rebuy Widget ID from your Rebuy dashboard
  4. Save changes

Rebuy will display AI-powered product recommendations on the pickup success page.

How do I set product dimensions?
  1. Go to the Product Sizes tab
  2. Find your product in the list
  3. Enter L x W x H dimensions in inches
  4. Optionally select a fixed locker size
  5. Click "Save All Changes"

Dimensions help LockerDrop automatically select the right locker size for orders.

Safety & Security

Are lockers secure?

Yes! Harbor Lockers feature:

  • Individual compartments with unique codes
  • Steel construction
  • 24/7 video surveillance
  • Access logging for all openings
  • Weather protection
What if a locker is damaged?
  • Do not use a damaged locker
  • Contact Harbor support immediately
  • Contact LockerDrop support for order reassignment
  • Document with photos if possible
Is customer information secure?

Yes, we follow industry best practices:

  • Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Limited access to customer information
  • Compliance with privacy regulations
  • Regular security audits
