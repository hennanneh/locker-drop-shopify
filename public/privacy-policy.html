Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 6, 2025

LockerDrop ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of merchants and their customers who use our Shopify application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal data.

1. Information We Collect

LockerDrop collects the minimum personal data necessary to provide our locker pickup service:

From Shopify Orders (Customer Data)

Data Type Purpose
Customer Name Personalize pickup notification emails
Customer Email Send pickup notification when package is ready
Customer Phone Send SMS pickup notification (optional)
Order Number Reference and tracking
Shipping Address Determine nearest locker locations at checkout

From Merchants (Store Data)

Data Type Purpose
Store Domain Identify your store and route orders
API Access Token Securely connect to your Shopify store
Product Information Determine appropriate locker sizes

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data exclusively for:

  • Order Fulfillment - Processing locker pickup orders
  • Customer Notifications - Sending pickup ready emails and SMS
  • Locker Assignment - Matching orders to appropriate locker sizes
  • Service Improvement - Analyzing usage to improve our service
We do NOT:
  • Sell personal data to third parties
  • Use data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Share data except as required to provide the locker service
  • Use data for automated decision-making with legal effects

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

  • Harbor Locker - Our locker provider, to facilitate package drop-off and pickup
  • Digital Ocean - Our hosting provider (data processor)
  • Email/SMS Providers - To send notifications on your behalf

All third parties are bound by data protection agreements.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary:

  • Active Orders - Data retained until order is completed
  • Completed Orders - Customer personal data is automatically deleted 90 days after pickup
  • Store Data - Retained while app is installed; deleted upon uninstallation

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in Transit - All data transmitted via TLS/SSL
  • Encryption at Rest - Database encryption using AES-256
  • Access Controls - Limited staff access to personal data
  • Audit Logging - All access to customer data is logged
  • Secure Backups - Encrypted automated backups

6. Your Rights

Merchants and customers have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of stored personal data
  • Correction - Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion - Request deletion of personal data
  • Portability - Receive data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection - Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@lockerdrop.it

7. Cookies and Tracking

LockerDrop uses only essential cookies required for app functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics that collect personal data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via the app dashboard or email.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

  • Email: privacy@lockerdrop.it
  • General Support: support@lockerdrop.it

12. Data Protection Agreement

By installing LockerDrop, merchants agree to our data processing practices as described in this policy. We act as a data processor on behalf of merchants (data controllers) for customer data.
